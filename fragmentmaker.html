Fragment Maker

About

This tool takes a long text and fragments it into shorter, single-line "reads" of 60 characters each. Reads overlap by 20 to 40 characters, allowing for the full text to be reassembled.

We chose 60 characters so reads could easily be fit on one line when printed landscape.


Instructions

Input the text you want to use in the top box and click the "Fragment!" button. Your fragmented text will appear in the bottom box and can be copy/pasted to a document for saving and printing.
